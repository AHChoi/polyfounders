HackPoly
Hackpoly is Cal Poly Pomona's annual collegiate hackathon put on by Poly Founders. It is a 24-hour software and hardware competition to build anything.
Bronco Startup Challenge
Bronco Startup Challenge is Cal Poly Pomona's annual event put on by Poly Founders. It's a 2-minute startup pitch competition that rewards students with over $10,000 in cash.
Workshops & Tech Talks
We host workshops with real business application, featured startup speakers, and Weekly "Refining the Idea" opportunities which is unique time set aside to share YOUR ideas to a room full of startup focused entrepreneurs.
Hack Nights
We host a quarterly competition where members make products in order to get their hands dirty as well as bond together as the Poly Founder community.
